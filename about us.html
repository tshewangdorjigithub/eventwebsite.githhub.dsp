WORLD of MUSIC

World of Music was first started in the year 2020 by the world music organization. It is one of the world's largest and most popular music festivals. Held annually in a different country, it attracts music lovers from all over the world who come to see some of the biggest shows of dance, DJ performances, and different genres of music. The festival's motto is to exchange different cultures and to stop racism. Over the years, the World of Music has brought lots of changes in cultural exchange and in stopping racism.Our belief is that music lovers don't have a bar. All they do is enjoy music and dance.So far we have organized fifteen shows in different countries.We can proudly say that all the shows held so far were massive success. We aim to reach out to global audieence and hence our festival is held in a diferent beautiful country each year. We wlcome you with warm hands in the most comforting embrace of music.
